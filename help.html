What is Kuso Physics?

Overview and How to Play

Welcome to Kuso Physics, a physics-based puzzle game where you guide a ball to its destination by manipulating the environment and using the laws of physics.

Game Overview

Kuso Physics is a challenging puzzle game that combines fun gameplay with real physics concepts. Your goal is to guide a ball from its starting position to the target destination by using gravity, momentum, friction, and other physics principles.

Key Features:

  • Physics-based puzzles with realistic ball movement
  • Multiple difficulty levels from beginner to expert
  • Custom level editor to create your own challenges
  • Educational articles about physics concepts
  • Share and play levels created by other players

How to Play

The gameplay is simple to understand but challenging to master:

1. Start the Ball

Press the Start button to begin the simulation and set the ball in motion.

2. Guide the Ball

Use Shake Mode to influence the ball's movement by shaking your device (on mobile) or moving your mouse (on desktop).

3. Reach the Target

Guide the ball to the red target ring to complete the level.

4. Reset if Needed

If the ball gets stuck or falls off the course, press Reset to try again.

Controls

Kuso Physics offers intuitive controls for both mobile and desktop users:

Mobile Controls:

  • Shake your device to apply force to the ball
  • Touch buttons for Start, Reset, and navigation
  • Pinch to zoom and rotate the view (where supported)

Desktop Controls:

  • Move your mouse rapidly to apply force to the ball (in Shake Mode)
  • Click buttons for Start, Reset, and navigation
  • Click and drag to rotate the view, scroll to zoom

Game Modes

Campaign Mode

Progress through increasingly difficult pre-designed levels that introduce new physics concepts.

Custom Levels

Play levels created by other users or create your own using the Level Editor.

Level Editor

Design your own physics puzzles with the built-in editor. Place platforms, set bounce and friction properties, define start and end points, and share your creation with others.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some helpful tips to improve your gameplay:

Use Momentum

Let the ball build up speed on downward slopes to help it climb upward sections.

Understand Bounce Properties

Pink/purple platforms have higher bounce properties than green ones. Use them to reach higher areas.

Control Your Force

Apply gentle forces when the ball needs precise guidance. Too much force can send it off course.

Study the Layout

Before starting, take a moment to analyze the level and plan your approach.

Learn from Physics Articles

Read the in-game physics articles to better understand the principles that govern the game mechanics.
